# Judge Guide

This guide covers how to use the World of Floorcraft tablet marking interface for competition judging.

## Getting Started

Navigate to the **Judge** page (`/judge`) on your tablet or device. This page does not require a platform account.

### Logging In

1. Enter the **Competition Code** (3-4 character code, e.g. "OSB") — the scrutineer or organizer will provide this
2. Enter the **Master Password** — shared with all judges for this competition
3. Enter your **Judge Number** — your assigned judge ID for this competition
4. Tap **Sign In**

Your session persists across page refreshes. You'll stay logged in until you log out or the session expires (24 hours).

---

## Waiting for a Round

After logging in, you'll see a **waiting screen** if no round is currently active. The scrutineer controls when rounds start. The page automatically checks for new rounds every few seconds.

---

## Marking Preliminary Rounds (Callbacks)

When the scrutineer starts a preliminary round, your screen will show a grid of competitor numbers.

### How to Mark

- **Tap once** to mark a couple (green = callback)
- **Tap again** to mark as "maybe" (yellow — visual reminder only, counts as unmarked if submitted)
- **Tap a third time** to unmark (back to default)

The screen shows:
- Your current mark count vs. the requested number of callbacks
- Couples grouped by heat (if multiple heats)

### Submitting

1. Tap **Submit** when you've finished marking
2. If your count doesn't match the requested callbacks, you'll see a confirmation dialog
3. After submitting, marks are greyed out

### Editing After Submit

If you need to change your marks:
1. Tap **Edit Marks**
2. Make your changes
3. Tap **Re-submit**

Changed marks are recorded as corrections in the system.

---

## Marking Final Rounds (Rankings)

In a final round, you rank all couples from 1st to last for each dance.

### How to Rank

1. Tap a competitor number to assign the next placement
2. Couples move from the number grid to the ranking list above
3. To remove a placement, tap the couple in the ranking list

### Multi-Dance Events

For events with multiple dances, use the **dance tabs** at the top to switch between dances. A checkmark appears on each tab when that dance is fully ranked.

### Submitting

- You must rank all couples for all dances before submitting
- After submitting, tap **Edit Rankings** to make changes

---

## Logging Out

Tap **Logout** in the top-right corner. This ends your session. The scrutineer can also end your session remotely if needed (e.g., to switch judges on a tablet).

---

## Tips

- **Use the maybe state** (yellow) during preliminary rounds to keep track of borderline couples, then decide before submitting
- **Don't worry about mistakes** — you can always edit and re-submit, and the scrutineer can also unlock your submission for correction
- **Keep your tablet charged** — sessions last up to 24 hours but depend on your device staying on
- **If the round disappears**, it means the scrutineer has advanced to the next round — wait for the new round to appear
